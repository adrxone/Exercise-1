My Top 5 Games Ever:

Image version of the full tier list at the bottom.

  1. Dark Souls
  2. NieR: Automata
  3. Destiny 2: Forsaken
  4. Elden Ring
  5. Terraria

Dark Souls

Great gameplay, great difficulty, free to go wherever, can see almost everything near the start.

NieR: Automata

Great story, good and unique gameplay, great characters, and great music. Is the main inspiration for me wanting to make games.

Destiny 2: Forsaken

Specific time period of Destiny 2. Large amount of content, hidden content, great gameplay, solid story, also when I was one of the best players in the world for PVP.

Elden Ring

Great gameplay, first soulslike open world, decent difficulty.

Terraria

Can be relaxing, can be stressful, nice progression system, nice building system, good gameplay. Is not 2D Minecraft.
